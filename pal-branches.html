PAL Branches

Praesidia Alliance operates across five regions of the South Pacific — providing locally based security expertise with consistent standards throughout our network.

Established 2011

Security You Can Count On, Wherever You Are

Since 2011, Praesidia Alliance has built a reputation as the South Pacific's most trusted specialist security provider. Our branch network spans Tonga, Samoa, Vanuatu, New Zealand and Australia — enabling us to serve clients across the region with local knowledge and rapid response capability.

Every PAL branch operates to the same service standards, using the same authorised product lines and trained technicians. Whether you are a government department, commercial enterprise or private individual, our branches deliver consistent, professional security solutions tailored to local conditions.
