Compliance & Assessment

Testing & Assessment

Ensuring your security and fire safety systems perform as designed — formal testing, certification and compliance reporting across the South Pacific.

Why Testing Matters

Systems That Are Installed Must Also Be Verified

Praesidia Alliance conducts testing and assessment of security and fire safety systems to ensure compliance and peak performance. Installing a system is only the first step — regular testing and formal assessment are essential to confirm that every component performs as intended when it is needed most.

Our technicians are trained in the testing of all major security and fire systems installed across the South Pacific. We produce formal written reports that satisfy the requirements of insurers, government regulators and building compliance programmes.

Services

Testing & Assessment Programmes

01
Intruder Alarm Testing
Full functional testing of intruder alarm systems — detectors, control panels, keypads, sirens and communication paths to the monitoring centre. Written pass/fail report produced.
02
Fire Alarm Testing & Certification
Comprehensive testing of fire detection systems including smoke and heat detectors, manual call points, control panels and alarm sounders. Certification documentation produced for regulatory and insurance purposes.
03
Access Control Assessment
Review and testing of electronic access control systems — readers, controllers, locking hardware and user credential databases — to confirm correct operation and identify vulnerabilities.
04
CCTV System Review
Assessment of CCTV coverage, camera positioning, recording quality, storage capacity and remote access. Recommendations provided where gaps or deficiencies are identified.
05
Security Risk Assessment
Formal assessment of your site's physical security posture, identifying risks, vulnerabilities and gaps against recognised security standards. A written report with prioritised recommendations is delivered.
06
Annual Compliance Reports
Scheduled annual testing visits with comprehensive written compliance reports — designed to satisfy insurance, government and corporate governance requirements.
