Privacy Policy

How Praesidia Alliance Limited collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Praesidia Alliance Limited, Reg No: 9005292, Nuku'alofa, Kingdom of Tonga.

1. Introduction

Praesidia Alliance Limited ("Praesidia Alliance", "we", "us" or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our business, visit our website or use our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information.

By using our website or engaging with our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Name and contact details (email address, telephone number, postal address)
  • Company or organisation name
  • Country of residence or operation
  • Enquiry details and correspondence
  • Service agreement information for clients

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and browser type when visiting our website
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website or search terms used to find our site
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6)

Information From Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties such as referrals from existing clients, authorised partner organisations or publicly available sources relevant to our legitimate business interests.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested information about our services
  • To provide, manage and deliver security services under a service agreement
  • To process transactions and manage billing
  • To communicate service updates, maintenance notices and important operational information
  • To improve our website and service delivery
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To protect against fraud, misuse of our services or unlawful activity

We will not use your personal information for direct marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery: With authorised subcontractors or partner organisations where necessary to provide the services you have requested, subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations
  • Legal compliance: Where required by law, court order or regulatory authority in any jurisdiction in which we operate
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition or sale of all or part of our business, subject to equivalent privacy protections
  • Protection of rights: Where disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of Praesidia Alliance, our clients or the public

5. Security

We take the security of personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction or alteration. These measures include secure storage systems, access controls and staff training.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you believe your personal information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at info@praesidia-alliance.com.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and help us understand how our site is used. Cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behaviour so we can improve the site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Client service records are retained for a minimum of 7 years following the end of a service engagement, in line with standard business and tax record-keeping requirements.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9 below. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: info@praesidia-alliance.com
  • Post: Praesidia Alliance Limited, Bypass Road, Ma'ufanga, Nuku'alofa, Kingdom of Tonga
  • Phone: +676 24 126

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
