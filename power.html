Power Protection Systems

Keeping your security infrastructure operational when the grid fails — UPS systems, battery backup and generator integration for the South Pacific environment.

PowerShield UPS

Security That Keeps Working When the Power Doesn't

Power outages are a frequent challenge across the South Pacific. Unreliable grid supply can render even the most sophisticated security system useless at the exact moment it is needed most. Praesidia Alliance supplies, installs and maintains UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems and backup power solutions to keep your security systems running through any outage.

We work with the PowerShield product range — a professional-grade UPS brand designed specifically for the demands of security and IT infrastructure. Our technicians size, specify and install the correct solution for your site, from a single camera circuit to a full-site security power installation.

What We Provide

Power Protection Services

A complete power protection offering — from initial consultancy through to ongoing maintenance of your backup power infrastructure.

01
UPS System Supply & Installation
We supply and professionally install PowerShield UPS units sized to protect your security infrastructure — intruder alarms, CCTV, access control and network equipment.
02
Battery Maintenance & Replacement
Scheduled battery testing, maintenance visits and replacement of degraded batteries to ensure your UPS delivers full backup capacity when needed.
03
Generator Interface
Integration of UPS systems with standby generators to provide seamless transition to generator power and extended runtime during prolonged outages.
04
Surge Protection
Transient voltage surge suppressors installed to protect sensitive security electronics from the power quality issues common in the South Pacific electrical environment.
05
System Monitoring
Remote monitoring of UPS health and battery status, with alerts to our operations centre when a unit switches to battery or a fault condition is detected.
06
Power Consultancy
Site assessment and power consultancy to identify vulnerabilities in your security system's power supply and recommend the right protection strategy.
