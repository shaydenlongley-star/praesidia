About Praesidia Alliance

15 Years of South Pacific Expertise

Selecting a security provider with extensive experience combined with local knowledge, specialist facilities, and a proven long-term track record is a smart choice.

Praesidia Alliance Limited has been operating across the South Pacific since 2011, building lasting relationships with clients in Tonga, Samoa, Vanuatu, New Zealand and Australia. Our team brings deep regional understanding that international security firms simply cannot match.

What We Do

Specialist Security Services

A complete suite of protective systems designed for the unique demands of the South Pacific region.

Intruder & Fire Alarms

Professional installation and monitoring of intruder detection and fire alarm systems for residential, commercial and industrial sites.

Video Surveillance

CCTV and IP camera networks with remote viewing. Geutebrück image alert network integration for critical and high-security facilities.

24/7 Monitoring

Round-the-clock alarm monitoring and management from our operations centre. SMS and app-based alert delivery to key personnel.

Access Control

Electronic locking, keycard and biometric access control systems. Authorised Gallagher Security integrators for enterprise installations.

GPS Tracking

Vehicle and asset GPS location systems via TrackNETMobi — live tracking to protect your fleet and high-value mobile assets.

Security Consultancy

Comprehensive security assessments, risk analysis and system design for new developments and existing commercial facilities.

Fire Suppression

Fire extinguisher supply, installation, maintenance and compliance testing. Smoke detector installation and 24/7 monitoring.

IP PBX Telephony

Modern IP telephone systems for business — scalable, cost-effective and integrated with your broader security infrastructure.

Network & Cabling

Structured data cabling, fibre optic networks and IT infrastructure installation from small offices to large commercial projects.
