About AJAX

The World's Leading Wireless Security System

AJAX Systems is a world-leading security brand renowned for its advanced wireless technology and professional-grade reliability. Praesidia Alliance are the authorised AJAX integrators for the South Pacific region, providing the full AJAX product range to residential, commercial and government clients across Tonga, Samoa, Vanuatu, New Zealand and Australia.

AJAX provides wireless security systems including intruder detection, fire alarms and smart home integration. Every system is designed for rapid installation without compromise on performance. The AJAX app provides real-time alerts, remote arm and disarm capability, and instant notification of any alarm event directly to your smartphone.

Product Range

AJAX Product Categories

Praesidia Alliance supply, install and maintain the full range of AJAX products. Every installation is configured and commissioned by our trained technicians.

01
Hub Control Panels
The central intelligence of every AJAX system — connects all devices, communicates with the monitoring centre and sends alerts to users via the AJAX app.
02
Motion Detectors
Indoor and outdoor PIR and dual-technology motion detectors, immune to false alarms, with pet immunity options available for residential installations.
03
Door & Window Sensors
Magnetic contact sensors for doors, windows, gates and safes. Instant tamper detection and wireless range up to 1700 metres in open field conditions.
04
Smoke & Heat Detectors
Wireless fire detection devices that integrate directly into the AJAX security ecosystem, delivering fire alerts through the same app and monitoring infrastructure.
05
Sirens & Strobes
Indoor and outdoor sirens with loud audible alerts and bright strobe lights. Wireless installation, tamper protection and backup battery for continued operation during power cuts.
06
Remote Controls & Keypads
Key fobs, keypads and readers for arming and disarming your system. Multiple user credentials supported for teams and families.
