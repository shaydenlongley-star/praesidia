Specialist Services

Beyond standard security installations, Praesidia Alliance offers a range of specialist programmes through authorised partnerships with world-leading security brands.

What We Offer

Purpose-Built for the South Pacific

Each specialist service has been developed to address the specific challenges of operating security infrastructure in island nations — from power instability and remote asset tracking to the need for round-the-clock professional monitoring. Our authorised partner relationships ensure you receive genuine, supported products backed by the original manufacturers.

AJAX Systems

Wireless intruder detection, fire alarms, smart home integration and real-time app alerts. Praesidia Alliance are authorised AJAX integrators for the South Pacific region.

Learn More

GPS Vehicle & Asset Tracking

Real-time fleet and asset monitoring via the TrackNETMobi platform. Set geo-fence alerts, review travel history and protect your mobile assets across all PAL regions.

Learn More

24/7 Monitoring Network

Our operations centre in Nuku'alofa runs around the clock. Every alarm event is received, assessed and actioned by trained monitoring staff with instant SMS and app-based alerts.

Learn More

Power Protection

UPS systems, battery maintenance, surge protection and generator interface solutions to keep your security infrastructure running during the frequent power outages common across the South Pacific.

Learn More

Cash In Transit

Secure cash collection and transport services across the Kingdom of Tonga. Trained personnel and documented chain of custody procedures protect your cash during every movement.

Testing & Assessment

Compliance testing and assessment for intruder alarms, fire systems, CCTV and access control. Our technicians produce formal reports to satisfy insurance and regulatory requirements.
