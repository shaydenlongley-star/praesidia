Cash In Transit

Protecting your cash and valuables during every movement — professional CIT services across the Kingdom of Tonga.

Secure CIT
Tonga CIT Services

Trusted Cash Transport Across the Kingdom

Praesidia Alliance provides secure cash-in-transit services across the Kingdom of Tonga. Our trained personnel and rigorous operating procedures protect your cash and valuables during transport between locations — whether from your premises to the bank, between branches, or for payroll deliveries.

Every CIT movement is supported by documented chain of custody procedures, ensuring complete accountability from collection to delivery. Regular scheduled runs or ad hoc movements can be arranged to suit your operational requirements.

What We Provide

CIT Service Capabilities

Our CIT service covers every stage of a secure cash movement from collection through to documented delivery and reconciliation.

01
Secure Cash Collection
Trained CIT personnel attend your premises to collect cash and valuables in tamper-evident security bags, with full documentation of every collection.
02
Transport to Bank
Secure transport of your cash directly to the bank, following variable routes and procedures to minimise predictability and risk throughout the journey.
03
Armed Escort Option
For higher-value movements, armed escort services are available to provide an additional layer of security throughout the transit process.
04
Chain of Custody Documentation
Full chain of custody documentation from collection to delivery, providing complete accountability and a clear audit trail for every movement.
05
Regular Scheduled Runs
Set scheduled collection days and times to suit your operational pattern, ensuring cash is moved efficiently and consistently without disruption to your business.
06
Ad Hoc Movements
Short-notice or one-off cash movements arranged quickly when circumstances require an unscheduled collection or delivery.
