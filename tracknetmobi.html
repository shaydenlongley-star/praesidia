TrackNETMobi Platform

Complete Visibility of Your Fleet and Assets

TrackNETMobi is Praesidia Alliance's GPS vehicle and asset tracking platform. Monitor your fleet in real time, set geo-fence alerts to be notified when vehicles leave authorised zones, view full travel history and protect high-value mobile assets from theft or misuse.

The platform is available across all PAL regions, making it ideal for organisations with operations spanning multiple islands. Whether you manage a delivery fleet, government vehicles, heavy equipment or high-value cargo, TrackNETMobi provides the visibility and control you need.

Platform Features

What TrackNETMobi Delivers

01
Real-Time Location
View the exact position of every tracked vehicle or asset on a live map, updated continuously throughout the day.
02
Geo-Fence Alerts
Define authorised zones for your vehicles. Receive instant alerts when a vehicle enters or exits a geo-fence boundary — ideal for theft detection and operational compliance.
03
Travel History & Reports
Review complete journey history for any vehicle. Generate detailed reports on distance, stops, idle time and route compliance for operational and compliance purposes.
04
Driver Behaviour Monitoring
Monitor speeding, harsh braking and other driver behaviour indicators. Improve safety standards and reduce vehicle wear across your fleet.
05
Asset Protection
Covert tracking units can be fitted to high-value equipment, cargo or non-powered assets to provide location awareness and theft recovery capability.
06
Fleet Management
Manage your entire fleet from one platform. Assign vehicles, manage drivers, schedule maintenance reminders and export data for management reporting.
