The Process

How Our Monitoring Works

Every alarm event follows a defined procedure from the moment it is received to final incident logging. Nothing is missed, and every step is documented.

01
Alarm Signal Received
An alarm event from your intruder, fire, CCTV or access control system is transmitted to our operations centre and logged immediately upon receipt.
02
Assessment by Trained Operator
A trained monitoring operator reviews the event, assesses the signal type and alarm zone, and determines the appropriate action.
03
Verification Procedure
Where applicable, the operator follows the verification procedure — including CCTV review, keyholde calls or cross-reference with other site sensors — before escalating.
04
SMS & App Alert to Key Personnel
Confirmed events trigger instant SMS and AJAX app notifications to your designated key contacts, providing full event details and site location.
05
Response Co-ordination
Where a physical response is required, our operators co-ordinate with the appropriate response parties including keyholders, security response personnel or police.
06
Incident Logging
Every event is fully documented in our incident management system, creating a permanent record for insurance, compliance and security review purposes.
